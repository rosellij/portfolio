Hello! My name is John Garces Roselli, and this is my portfolio site! Currently, I am a student at both the School of Information and the School of Music at the University of Michigan, studying Information (User Experience concentration) and Multidisciplinary Studies (with Trombone as a primary instrument) respectively.

I am from Charlotte, North Carolina, and I am currently 21 years old! That's crazy!


I do some other neat things outside of UX Design and playing trombone! I've helped teach other students Python, helped lay the groundwork for a massive music education program in Detroit, been a driver for a driverless bus with an XBox controller, made some neat cross-disciplinary avant-garde performance art, lived in Gibraltar, UK, and have been able to go to a whole bunch of crazy different places!

Aside from all that craziness, I love to cook; recently, I've been perfecting my recipes for Rigatoni Buttera and Detroit Style Pizza. I love cooking as, just like with music, it gives me an outlet to share something I love, something joyous with the people I care about. I am, first and foremost, a people-driven person, and I look forward to bringing that human-centered attitude to the classroom and the workplace every day.

Ask me about any of it, honestly! I hope hearing about some of my crazy life bits inspires you to reflect on and talk about your crazy life bits! I'd love to hear about 'em sometime!
